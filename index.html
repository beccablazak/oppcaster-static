OPPCASTER

Helping local, minority, and women owned businesses learn about contracting opportunities with the City.

Transparent

All projects for which the City is soliciting quotes will appear on the website. Visitors can browse current and past opportunities to understand what the City is purchasing.

Inclusive

In the past, City purchasers would contact known vendors to solicit quotes for small contracts, meaning many businesses were out of the loop. With OppCaster, any interested vendor can sign up to receive notifications of new opportunities.

Efficient

OppCaster saves time and money. City purchasers no longer need to make multiple phone calls to get quote for a project. They enter the information once and press a button to share the quote request to every interested business. More vendors at the table means lower prices.

Smaller Dollar Procurement in
South Bend, Ind.

OppCaster is helping the city of South Bend grow its minority, women, and local owned businesses while improving competition.
